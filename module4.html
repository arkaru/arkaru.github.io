About Module 4

Welcome to the module "Connecting Global Issues to Science Concepts"! In this module, we explore the exciting intersection of science and real-world challenges. Get ready to discover how current global issues could be understood using basic science concepts. Using an example, I will show you how to identify the basic science concepts related to a pressing global issue like climate change. In this module, you will learn how to connect pressing problems our world faces to classroom science concepts, empowering your students to become active global citizens. Let's embark on this meaningful journey together and inspire the next generation of scientific thinkers and changemakers.
