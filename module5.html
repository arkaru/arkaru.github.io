About Module 5

Welcome to the module on "Crafting Globally Competent Science Lesson Plans." In this module, we dive into the art of designing science lessons that go beyond the textbook, fostering global awareness and cultural understanding. You'll learn how to infuse your lessons with real-world connections, cross-cultural perspectives, and sustainable practices. By the end of this module, you'll be equipped with the skills to create engaging and impactful science lessons that empower your students to become global citizens, equipped to tackle the challenges our world faces. Get ready to embark on a journey of creativity, exploration, and global competence as we reshape science education for a brighter future..
