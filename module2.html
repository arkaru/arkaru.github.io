About Module 2

Welcome to the module on "Globally Competent Skills for Teaching Science." Throughout this module, we will explore the essential skills that empower educators to foster a global perspective in their science classrooms. From promoting cultural understanding and critical-self awareness to developing critical thinking and collaboration skills, this module will introduce you to the skills needed to cultivate globally competent students. By the end of this module, you will possess an understanding of the necessary skills to inspire and empower the next generation of scientifically literate global citizens, capable of addressing the complex challenges our world faces. Get ready to enhance your teaching practice and embark on a journey towards global competence in science education.
