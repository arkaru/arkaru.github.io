About Module 3

Welcome to the module "Connecting Science Concepts to Global Issues"! In this module, we explore the exciting intersection of science and real-world challenges. Get ready to discover how basic science concepts could be applied to understand and address global issues. Using an example, I will show you how to connect basic science concepts to global challenges like nuclear explosions, glacier melting, etc. In this module, you will learn how to connect science concepts to the pressing problems our world faces, empowering your students to become active global citizens. Let's embark on this meaningful journey together and inspire the next generation of scientific thinkers and changemakers.
