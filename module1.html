About Module 1

Welcome to the module, "Introduction to Global Competency in Science." In this module, we dive into the significance of global competency in science education. As teachers, we play a crucial role in shaping our students' understanding of the world and their place in it. By exploring why global competency is essential for our young learners, this module will inspire you to empower your students to become informed global citizens. We'll delve into pressing global challenges, Sustainable Development Goals, and why it is crucial to integrate global perspectives into your science lessons. Join us on this exciting journey as we strengthen our students' scientific knowledge and nurture their curiosity about the world we live in.
